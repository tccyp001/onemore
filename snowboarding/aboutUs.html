ABOUT US

1MORE Inc., was established in 2013 and is headquartered in Shenzhen China, with origins focusing on acoustic design and development, smart software and wearable products. 1MORE is what we do. We always take one more step to provide the highest quality and performance. 1MORE devotes itself to delivering products with cutting edge designs and unbeatable prices.

1MORE is cultivating long term strategic partnerships with vital suppliers. Through joint component research and development, our bond is strengthened. Incorporating fine materials and sophisticated technology, while pursuing the highest quality standards, 1MORE is blending exhilarating fashion with comfort, resulting in a robust product experience.

About 1More

Fashionably designed with acoustics at heart

At 1MORE, our mission is to use the latest technology to create premium quality audio devices at prices everyone can afford, look stylish wearing, and feel good about purchasing. This has been our mission ever since we came out with our first product, the Mi piston headphones, and we have been adhering to these principles ever since. We use environmentally-friendly production processes, premium craftsmanship, fashionable design, and non-toxic materials in every product we build.

TECHNOLOGY

Aerospace-grade metal composite diaphragm

The patented triple layer diaphragm design allows a listener to hear music as the musician original intended. The result is clean treble, immaculate mid-range, and powerful bass.

Innovation

1MORE holds a number of patents for its headphone innovations. These include: scratch and slip resistant CD-surface like textured stripes on metal surfaces of the headphones; a dual balanced damping system, and a triple-layer composite diaphragm. 1MORE also originated the intelligent control box, which is compatible with iOS and Android.
